**Income reports**: 8-12 lines (total + 7-11 breakdown lines)  
**Educational threads**: 3-5 tweets typically  
**Provocative**: 2-4 lines (short and punchy)  
**How-tos**: 3-7 steps  
**Personal**: 2-3 lines (very brief)
