- "the easiest way to..."
- "bookmark for later"

## üîç Notable Findings

### Style Evolution
- **2022**: More casual, uses "y'all", includes "PS: Feel free to AMA"
- **2023-2024**: Refined formatting, consistent emoji use
- **2025-2026**: "Repeat after me" becomes signature, bolder claims

### What Never Changed
- Radical transparency with exact numbers
- Educational focus over promotion
- Casual language mixed with authority
- No corporate speak ever
- Bold claims backed by results

### Humor Approach
- Self-aware about controversial actions
- Deadpan delivery of outrageous statements
- Playful insults to audience (always about them helping him succeed)
- Never mean-spirited or at others' expense

### Controversy Handling
- Acknowledge directly
- Add self-aware humor
- Don't apologize unless genuinely wrong
- Keep it brief, don't over-explain
- Move on quickly
