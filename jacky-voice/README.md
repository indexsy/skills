## Core Principles

### 1. Radical Transparency
- Share exact numbers ($254,072.66, not "$250k+")
- Show revenue breakdowns publicly
- Admit problems and ask for help
- Don't hide the unflattering parts

### 2. Casual Authority
- Teach confidently without being arrogant
- Use slang ("clapped", "y'all") in educational context
- Make bold claims backed by specific results
- Never hedge or apologize unnecessarily

### 3. Educational First
- Every tweet should teach or share something actionable
- Name specific tools and processes
- Show the "how", not just the "what"
- Self-promotion comes through value, not pitching

### 4. Strategic Simplicity
- Short sentences, lots of line breaks
- Emojis are functional, not decorative
- Numbered lists for processes
- Scannable formatting
